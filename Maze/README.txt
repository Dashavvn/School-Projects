Starter files for lab7
(CMSC 162, s19, Blaheta)

Basically just a complete implementation of Labs 2 and 3.  Executables are
test_Location and test_Maze (unit testing).

To compile:
	compile Location.cpp test_Location.u -o test_Location
	compile Location.cpp Maze.cpp test_Maze.u -o test_Maze

Or just:
	make

To run unit tests:
	./test_Location
	./test_Maze
